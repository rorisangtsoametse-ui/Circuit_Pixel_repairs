About Circuit Pixel Repairs

Circuit Pixel Repairs is a trusted, locally-owned electronics repair service based in Maseru, Lesotho. With a commitment to excellence, we specialize in the diagnosis and repair of televisions and household appliances, ensuring reliable solutions for all your electronic needs.

Our team of experienced technicians brings years of hands-on expertise in repairing LED, LCD, and Smart TVs, along with a wide range of home appliances. We pride ourselves on delivering honest, transparent service with competitive pricing and a focus on customer satisfaction.
